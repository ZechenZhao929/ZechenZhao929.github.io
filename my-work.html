Fashion Image Editing with Diffusion Models

I chose to explore diffusion models for fashion image editing because they offer a promising alternative to traditional generative models like GANs. My goal was to address challenges in generating realistic and diverse fashion images without requiring manual adjustments or extensive model training for each dataset.

To achieve this, I combined a diffusion model with a Vision Transformer (ViT)-based classifier that uses an attention-pooling mechanism. This approach allows for precise and flexible modifications of multiple fashion attributes in a single framework. The results demonstrated that this method significantly improves the quality and accuracy of generated images, outperforming existing models in fashion image manipulation.
